🏛️ Heat Action Plan — Preventive Information
🌍 Government & Community Actions to Reduce Heatwave Impact
🏥
Health Services
Set up cooling centres in hospitals and public buildings. Increase ambulance availability. Train health workers to identify heat stroke symptoms quickly.
👷
Outdoor Workers
Restrict heavy outdoor work between 11 AM–4 PM during High risk days. Provide shade, clean water every 15 mins, and mandatory rest breaks.
🌾
Farmers & Agriculture
Schedule irrigation in early morning. Provide crop covering materials. Issue district-level heatwave SMS alerts to registered farmers automatically.
👴
Vulnerable Populations
Door-to-door wellness checks for elderly and children. Community volunteers assigned to high-risk neighbourhoods in summer months.
📡
Early Warning Broadcast
Automatic alerts via All India Radio, Doordarshan, and State Emergency Management Authority (SEMA). 48-hour advance warning targets.
🏗️
Urban Heat Mitigation
Cool roofs, reflective pavement, and green corridors. Night-time sprinkler activation in public areas during extreme heat events.
